About Us

Upping community knowledge

As part of our mission to elevate the practice of tech related activities, we offer bootcamps that brings the insights from our team to life for people of all ages open to be educated. Our bootcamps help you harness the power of design thinking.

Bonding with your type

As part of our “Have fun and be productive” core value, our bootcamps are spiced with multiple breakout sessions, bonding and team activities to make learning easy for teams and individuals involved.

Intensive training sessions

Actual learning is not left out as we teach individuals how to function as teams and work together towards common goals. Hackatons, design sprints, etc are not left out of training outlines.
